ABOUT US

We are team ENIGMA.

We are developing a Companion android application which helps women of rural areas to find a tutor for themselves. The app will have details of various tutors near them. App will have login pages, separate for tutor and students. Rural areas women will have an account for the app which they can access from their own devices. They can find nearest tutors using their location. Some qualified people in that area who are interested in teaching can sign up on their devices and get themselves a job. Some can do it for free of cost if they can. Tutors and students can then connect to each other. This will help women in rural area get educated and in spread of education.
